📖 How to Play The Category Chaos Game

🎯 Game Objective

Conquer the most territories by winning duels against your opponents!

🎮 Setup

  1. Enter Player Names: Add 8 players
  2. Random Topics: Each player starts with 3 random territories
  3. Categories: There are 24 different categories across the grid

⚔️ How Duels Work

  1. Start Duel: Click "Start Duel" to randomly select a matchup
  2. Countdown: 3-2-1 countdown before the duel begins
  3. Identify the Image: Take turns identifying the images on the screen
  4. Answering Correctly: Get an answer right and play passes on to your opponent
  5. Guessing: There's no penalty for wrong answers, so just keep guessing!
  6. Pass: If you really don't know an answer you can say pass to move onto the next image, but it will still be your turn!
  7. Timers: Each player has 25 seconds total
    • ⚠️ Yellow warning at 10 seconds
    • 🔴 Red alert at 5 seconds

✨ Power-Ups and Bonuses

  1. 🎲 Double or Nothing: Coin flip for double the territories or -1!
  2. ⏱️ Time Freeze: +5 seconds for the next duel!
  3. 🔄 Category Swap: Swap one category with another!
  4. 🛡️ Shield: Protect your territory!

🏆 Winning

  • ⏰ Whoever's clock runs out first loses the duel
  • ✅ If you finish all the images, the Player with MORE TIME remaining wins the duel
  • 🎯 The winner takes over that player's space on the grid
  • 🔁 The winner can choose to keep playing or stop and launch a new random duel
    • If you choose to continue, you can PICK an adjacent topic that you want to challenge!
  • 🏁 Player with the most territories wins!

💡 Pro Tips

  • ⚡ Speed matters! Answer quickly to save time
  • ⏭️ Pass strategically: Don't waste time guessing if you really don't know it
  • 🧠 Know your categories: Win duels to challenge in your strong areas
  • ⏱️ Watch the timer: Manage your time wisely
  • 🔥 Power-Ups: Use power-ups to your advantage
